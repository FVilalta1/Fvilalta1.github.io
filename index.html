About

Engineering meets governance.

As a Cyber Data Protection Manager at Grant Thornton, I architect and implement enterprise-grade security solutions within the Microsoft Security stack for a range of clients, including Fortune 200 conglomerates and Tier 1 global banks. My approach bridges the gap between technical engineering and strategic governance — from designing future-state security operating models to implementing advanced Insider Threat Management and Purview data security workflows.

Currently pursuing an M.S. in Cybersecurity at Georgia Tech, I focus on the intersection of data security, compliance, and proactive threat defense. I'm passionate about translating complex regulatory requirements into robust, automated technical controls — leveraging AI tools like Security Copilot so organizations can protect their most sensitive data while maintaining operational efficiency at scale.
